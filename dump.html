Hydrofoiling

The magic carpet ride! Learning foiling behind the boat to start progressing onto kitesurf foiling or wing surf foiling. One of the easiest ways to learn how to foil is behind a boat. Your goal might be to wing foil, kite foil or even surf foil. Starting behind a boat will make your progression a lot faster.
